Schedule Plurinational Bayes 2025

The agenda will be structured as follows:

  • October 15: Focused on Bayesian topics.
  • October 16: Dedicated to machine learning (potentially Bayesian) topics.
  • October 17: Under definition, with opportunities for morning activities and networking in the afternoon.

    • The tentative schedule for the first two days includes:

  • 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM: Keynote presentations
  • 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM: Lunch (+ posters)
  • 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM: Parallel workshops
  • 3:30 PM – 5:00 PM: Parallel sessions for individual talks

Scholarship Métodos + Sadosky


The \(\text{Bayesian Methods Labs}\) and the \(\text{Sadosky Foundation}\) provide partial scholarships to all expositors of the Plurinational Bayesian Congress (talks or posters). Both institutions have made available the same amount of resources for this scholarship. The total amount will be divided in equal parts, and will be delivered in person during the activities in Santiago del Estero.

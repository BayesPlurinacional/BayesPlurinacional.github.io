To organize your trip (accommodation, meals, etc.), contact the Bayes Plurinational team.
We will use the following WhatsApp group for communications during the event.

Location:

The event will be held at the Universidad Nacional de Salta.

Av. Bolivia 5150, Salta City, Argentina.

Accommodations:

In all cases, you must confirm your place by contacting the Bayes Plurinational team.

1. House for speakers.

Casa Montaña. Address: Santiago del Estero 282, Salta. (link to Google Maps)

2. Hotel for speakers.

Gran Hotel Presidente. Address: Av. Belgrano 353, Salta. (link to Google Maps)

3. Hostel for students.
To request a place, please fill in the form https://bayesplurinacional.org/link/es/HospedajeUNSa.html

Universidad Nacional de Salta. Address: Av. Bolivia 5150, Salta. (link to the UNSa web)
It has two rooms with 18 beds each (36 beds in total). You need to bring your own bedding (sheets).

4. Camping facilities.
To request a place, please fill in the form https://bayesplurinacional.org/link/es/HospedajeUNSa.html

Universidad Nacional de Salta. Address: Av. Bolivia 5150, Salta. (link to the UNSa web)
The tents are set up under a roof and have good sanitary facilities.

Location:

The activities of the Congress will be carried out in the Nodo Tecnológico located within the La Isla Industrial Park, in the city of La Banda, Santiago del Estero, Argentina.

Address: Los Molinos Avenue, G4300, La Banda, Santiago del Estero.

Transport:

One of the alternatives is the Tren Al Desarrollo SDE, Santiago del Estero.

The urban train of Santiago del Estero, or Tren al Desarrollo, runs along the Mitre Line tracks between the city of Santiago del Estero and La Banda, in Argentina. It starts from the Centro Station near the Fórum to the Nodo Station.

Cost: Free.

Frequency: currently the frequency is every 60 minutes.

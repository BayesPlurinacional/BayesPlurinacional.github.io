Bayesian Plurinational School-Congress 2024

The Bayesian Plurinational 2024 event was successfully held from August 6 to 10 at the National University of Salta, Argentina, with the support of the Latin American Artificial Intelligence organization, Khipu. We have already started forming teams to organize next year's activities, which will be held in Chile or Colombia, among other proposed countries.

  • Videos. You can see and hear the presentations on our YouTube Channel.
  • Materials. The presentation's PDF and other study materials can be found in our Google Drive.

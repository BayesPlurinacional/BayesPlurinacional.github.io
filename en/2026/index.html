PARAGUAY 2026 "Evidence for Action"

Bayes Plurinacional PARAGUAY 2026 — If collaboration is the basic Bayesian rule for community benefit, how can we work together sustainably for Latin American scientific and technological development? A new congress with Bayesian evidence for action and decision-making. Bayes in Science, Industry, Education, and Society. We will feature: keynote lectures, debates, posters, talks, and the Causal Bayesian Inference course on Bayesian intelligence and its applications across the sciences.

See you from August 18 to 20 in Paraguay. Welcome to our community!

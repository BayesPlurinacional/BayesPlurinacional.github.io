Congress Team

Scope: Comprehensive management of the annual in-person event. This team is responsible for logistical planning, coordination with the local venue, and operational execution of the congress. Its mission is to ensure the event takes place on the scheduled dates, with the necessary infrastructure and aligned with community and ISBA standards.

Communication Team

Scope: Building and managing the public and internal identity of Bayes Plurinacional. This area defines the narrative, visual branding, and outreach channels. Its goal is to raise the community's visibility, promote its activities, and highlight its members to society and industry.

  • Lead: Hugo Cristo Sant Anna
  • Associates: Alex Cavalcanti / Neder, Camila
  • Members: Verte, Xana; Alvarez, Facundo Nicolás; Moffatt, Luciano

Institutional Team

Scope: Legal formalization and political structure of the community. Its role is to provide Bayes Plurinacional with a legal framework to operate (receive funds, sign agreements) and define its governance model. It also manages relations with other entities such as ISBA.

  • Lead: Camacho, Mireya / Andrés Cervantes
  • Associate: Moffatt, Luciano
  • Members: Landfried, Gustavo; Oliveira, Anderson; Martinez, Domingo; Neder, Camila

Funding Team

Scope: Developing the economic sustainability of the organization. This team designs and executes strategies to obtain resources, both through traditional means (grants, subsidies) and by creating business models based on professional services that leverage members' expertise.

  • Lead: Verte, Oxana
  • Associate: Camacho, Mireya
  • Members: Moffatt, Luciano; Neder, Camila; Landfried, Gustavo

Education Team

Scope: Design and oversight of the academic offerings. This team establishes the community's educational framework, ensuring the quality and pedagogical coherence of courses and workshops. Its main goal this year is to consolidate the Bayesian School and deliver in-person certification.

  • Lead: Landfried, Gustavo
  • Associates: Sergio Hernández; Cabezas González, Alberto

Technical Team

Scope: Digital infrastructure support and technological tools. This area ensures the community has the platforms needed to operate, collaborate, and store information. It acts as cross-cutting support, providing the web, repositories, databases, and coordination tools for all other teams.

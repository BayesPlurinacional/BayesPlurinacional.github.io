Schedule Plurinational Bayes 2024

During the Plurinational Bayes Congress 2023 it was proposed as an objective to strengthen the offer of training in research and/or application of Bayesian inference, through the development of workshops taught by specialists from our region, giving rise to the creation of the Plurinational Bayes School-Congress 2024. The Plurinational Bayes School-Congress 2024 will be held at the National University of Salta, Argentina. During 3 days (Tuesday 6 to Thursday 8) we will share various activities, including several practical workshops at all levels, exhibitions, panel discussions, poster sessions, among others. On Friday morning we will carry out co-working activities and in the afternoon social activities in the city of Salta.

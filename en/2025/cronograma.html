Instructions for participants

Extended abstracts

Extended abstracts must be a maximum of 3 pages, indicating title, authors, affiliations, contact email, introduction, methodology, main findings, conclusions, and figures, tables and equations as appropriate.

Please send a compressed editable file to Bayes Plurinacional: bayesplurinacional@gmail.com before the start of the Conference, following the style of this PDF. We recommend using the following LaTeX template or Word.

Oral presentations

We recommend preparing presentations in PowerPoint or a similar software to hand in at the start of the session. If you wish, you may upload it to our Drive platform on the day of the Conference. 15 Minutes talk + 5 questions. More information with the Organizers.

Posters

Posters must be A0 size, portrait orientation. Spanish is preferred, or English. Font size ≥ 16. Consider the suggestions in the previous sections for a clear poster. Ideally, hang your poster on Tuesday at the Universidad Externado de Colombia. More information with the Organizers.

The Bayes Plurinacional community, the Bayesian Methods Laboratories, the Interdisciplinary AI Center UNSAM and the Universidad Nacional del Este offer three training modalities in Causal Bayesian Inference. The training, certification and introduction modalities have limited seats and a cost. Open study materials are available on GitHub for those who wish to prepare for certification independently.

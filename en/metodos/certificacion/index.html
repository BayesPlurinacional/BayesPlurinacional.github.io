Description


The International Certification in Causal Bayesian Inference will take place in person during the annual event of the Bayes Plurinacional community, coordinated by the Bayesian Methods Laboratories, the Interdisciplinary AI Center UNSAM and the Universidad Nacional del Este.

The event takes place from August 18 to 20, 2026 at the Universidad Nacional del Este, Paraguay.

Exam format

The exam consists of multiple-choice questions with belief distributions and written justifications. It evaluates understanding of the foundations of causal Bayesian inference covered in the open IBC1 course.

Course materials are freely available at github.com/MetodosBayesianos/IBC1.2026.1. It is not required to have completed the synchronous training to take the certification exam.
